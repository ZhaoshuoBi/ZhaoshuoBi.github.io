The Origins of the Avant Garde
Poster Design (11" x 17")

TOOLS
Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop


BACKGROUND
Type, image, and organization are the building blocks of visual communication. This project aimed to practice the use of text and images to communicate informational and emotional content. This poster advertises the avant garde conference. I was given a large amount of unformatted text and need to organize and fit them into the poster, which also need to include a picture choosen from a set of avant garde architecture
