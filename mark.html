Mark

FIELD: User-Centered Design, Web Design   ROLE: Team Leader/Coder
TAG: Interaction Design class group project   DURATION: Eight Weeks  





Brief
Have you ever drive by a place that attracts your eye, and you want to visit on your next trip, but never happened because you can't find it anymore? Our app, the Mark, allows you to mark your favorite place with one tap so you can revisit them whenever you want.  



The Design Idea
The app will have four basic characteristics. First, users are able to save the current location while ensuring safity (by tapping, voice control, gesture, etc.). Second, users are able to edit the saved location and add notes. Third, users can share their locations to their friends. Last, users can be nevigated to saved location without hassle. The app requires the Location permission of the phone which allowed users save the current location. Due to the safity concern, user will be able to add notes of the saved location after friving. Considering most of people would need to have a nevigation app opened on the phone, our app need to be able to have voice command and/or gesture control (needs "display over other apps" permission on Andriod).
